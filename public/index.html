🎯 Portland Kia Sales Playbook

🗺️ Portland Buyer Segments

  • NE/SE Portland Young Professionals – Eco-conscious, love EVs. Push EV6, Niro EV, Soul.
  • Beaverton/Hillsboro Families – Need space & safety. Push Sorento, Telluride, Carnival.
  • SW Portland Commuters – Fuel efficiency. Push Forte, Sportage Hybrid, Niro HEV.
  • Gresham/East County – Value-focused. Push Rio, Forte, Sportage with strong financing.
  • Lake Oswego / West Linn – Premium buyers. Push EV6 GT-Line, Telluride SX-P.

🔥 Hot Lead Signals

  • Has a specific vehicle in mind
  • Mentions being pre-approved or cash buyer
  • Current car is broken, sold, or lease ending
  • Asking about availability / color options
  • Has visited the lot before
  • Timeline: "this week" or "ASAP"

💬 Killer Opening Lines

  • "Hey [Name]! Saw you were checking out the EV6 — Portland just got a new state EV rebate, want me to run the numbers?"
  • "The Telluride you looked at has only 2 left on our lot. Want to hold one?"
  • "With gas prices in Portland averaging $4/gal, the Sportage Hybrid pays for itself fast!"

🛡️ Objection Busters

  • "I'm just looking" → "No pressure! What would need to be true for you to pull the trigger today?"
  • "Too expensive" → "What's your monthly budget? I have financing starting at $299/mo."
  • "Need to think about it" → "Totally fair — what's the #1 thing holding you back?"
  • "My spouse needs to see it" → "Great — want to bring them in today? We have refreshments!"

⚡ 5-Step Fast Qualifier

  1. What brings you in / what caught your eye?
  2. What are you driving now — and what do you love/hate about it?
  3. What's your must-have in your next car?
  4. Are you looking to own or lease, and have you looked at financing yet?
  5. If we found the perfect car today, is there anything stopping you from driving home in it?

🌧️ Portland EV Advantage

  • Oregon Clean Vehicle Rebate: up to $7,500
  • Federal Tax Credit: up to $7,500 on eligible EVs
  • PGE / Pacific Power EV charging incentives
  • Portland has 500+ public chargers
  • Kia EV6 rated #1 EV under $45k by Car & Driver

⚠️ Incentive amounts subject to change — verify current figures at oregoncleanvehiclerebate.org and IRS.gov before quoting customers.
